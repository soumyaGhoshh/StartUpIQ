Project Details

About StartupIQ

StartupIQ is an AI-powered business planning platform that helps transform ideas into executable business plans using machine learning and real-time data analysis.

Key Features

  • AI-generated business blueprints
  • Real-time market insights
  • Financial and investment forecasting
  • Pitch-ready documentation
  • Custom template generator
