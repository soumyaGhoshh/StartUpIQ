Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15th May 2025

Last Revised: 3rd June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, mobile number, and password (hashed)
  • Profile Data: Optional company information and profile picture
  • Contact Details: Email addresses and phone numbers provided for support

1.2 Business Content

  • AI-generated business plans and related documents
  • User-uploaded materials and customization inputs
  • Document metadata including creation dates and edit history

1.3 Technical Data

  • IP addresses, device information, and browser type
  • Usage patterns through Google Analytics (anonymized where possible)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6)

2. Use of Information

We process personal data for the following lawful bases and purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • User authentication and account management
  • AI model training and service improvement (anonymized datasets)
  • Document storage and retrieval operations

2.2 Communications

  • Service-related notifications and security alerts
  • Product updates and feature announcements (opt-out available)
  • Response to support requests and inquiries

2.3 Legal Compliance

  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring
  • Response to legal requests and court orders
  • Enforcement of our Terms of Service

3. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption using TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls and audit logging
  • SOC 2-compliant infrastructure monitoring

While we implement industry-standard measures, no electronic storage method is 100% secure. We encourage users to enable two-factor authentication and use strong passwords.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We share data with these authorized processors:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Google Firebase (Cloud Storage and Authentication)
  • Google Gemini (AI Processing Services)
  • Stripe (Payment Processing) - PCI DSS compliant

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect:

  • Our rights and property
  • User safety or public safety
  • Compliance with legal process

4.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Article 15 GDPR)
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data (Article 16 GDPR)
  • Request erasure of data ("Right to be Forgotten", Article 17 GDPR)
  • Restrict processing of your data (Article 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Article 20 GDPR)
  • Object to processing (Article 21 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, visit your account dashboard or contact our Data Protection Officer at [DPO Email]. Requests will be processed within 30 calendar days. We may require identity verification for security purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use these cookie categories:

  • Essential: Session cookies for authentication
  • Preference: Language and theme settings
  • Analytical: Google Analytics (GA4) with IP anonymization
  • Marketing: Conversion tracking (opt-in required)

Manage preferences via our Cookie Consent Manager or browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect service functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain data:

  • Active accounts: Until deletion request
  • Inactive accounts: 24 months from last login
  • Business documents: 12 months from last access
  • Legal records: 7 years for compliance

8. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in:

  • Google Cloud regions in the United States and EU
  • Third-party processors in GDPR-compliant jurisdictions

We use EU Standard Contractual Clauses for international data transfers.
